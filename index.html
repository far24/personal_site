Fahim Ahmed

Bio

Fahim Ahmed studies vehicle routing problem and their applications in supply chain logistics at UofSCTransportation Group. His research focuses on real-time vehicle routing planning under disruption scenrios. He works in a reasearch group at UofSC that frequently collaborates with researchers who studies transportation aspects of the supply chain logistics.
